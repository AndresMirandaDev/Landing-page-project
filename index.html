Welcome to Danielssons Sanering AB

Danielssons Sanering AB is a well-established and complete construction company that performs all common construction work during demolition, remediation and drilling. We help property owners, tenant-owner associations and companies in Stockholm and the surrounding area. No job is too small or big for us.

About our company.

Danielssons Sanering AB carries out overall commitments as a turnkey contractor for minor renovations.
Whether you need to tear down, clean up, or make holes, Danielssons Sanering AB will help you all the way.
After many years in the construction industry, we have an established network of contacts of other serious craftsmen in all professional groups with whom we collaborate regularly.
Our employees have broad skills and never leave a job half done.

Danielssons Sanering AB has all the necessary certificates and qualifications in asbestos abatement and drilling. We use quality materials from the leading suppliers in each business area for long-term customer solutions.
